About SEG-Y Format

SEG-Y (Society of Exploration Geophysicists) is the industry standard for storing seismic reflection data. A single SEG-Y file can contain millions of amplitude measurements representing sound waves reflected from rock layers deep underground.

Our tools respect the full specification including textual headers (3200 bytes), binary headers (400 bytes), and trace headers (240 bytes per trace) with support for both IEEE floating point and fixed-point formats.
