## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Key Risks

**Market Risks**:
- **Competition**: Established players may respond
- **Adoption**: Users may prefer existing solutions
- **Differentiation**: May be seen as "just another visualization tool"

**Technical Risks**:
- **Complexity**: Multi-domain platform increases complexity
- **Performance**: Browser limitations may constrain capabilities
- **Scalability**: Large datasets may challenge architecture

**Operational Risks**:
- **Team**: May need additional expertise (ML, 3D, domain experts)
- **Support**: Multiple domains increase support burden
- **Resources**: Spreading too thin across domains

### Mitigation Strategies

**Market Mitigation**:
- **Focus on web-based advantage** (no installation, accessible)
- **Target underserved segments** (education, small business, developing regions)
- **Build community** around open-source components
- **Continuous innovation** with ML and advanced features

**Technical Mitigation**:
- **Iterative development** (start simple, add complexity gradually)
- **Modular architecture** (components can be added/removed)
- **Performance monitoring** (continuous optimization)
- **User feedback** (validate technical decisions with users)

**Operational Mitigation**:
- **Hire strategically** (generalists + targeted specialists)
- **Community support** (forums, documentation, peer support)
- **Focus on core domains** (don't expand too quickly)
- **Partnerships** (leverage external expertise)
